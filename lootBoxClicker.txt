Shinies

		on click:log Woop
		movement:anywhere
		frequency:60
		frequency variation:30

		on click:

			$amount=max(7,random(bunnies:ps*60*1,loot:ps*60*3))*1
			toast The lucky bunny grants you<//><b>[$amount] loot</b>.

		end
